# D&G Email Bot Plan

Goal: reply naturally to new customer emails, collect the right details, and move leads toward a free on-site estimate.

## Launch Mode

Start in draft mode. The bot should create Gmail drafts for review, not send automatically.

Move to auto-send only after the replies sound right for real customers.

## Lead Intake

When a new email arrives, identify whether it is:

- New lead asking for landscaping, masonry, lawn, patio, walkway, sod, cleanup, or maintenance
- Existing customer follow-up
- Vendor/spam/irrelevant
- Urgent issue that needs human review

## Ideal Reply

Tone: warm, professional, natural, confident, not robotic.

Reply goals:

- Thank the customer
- Confirm D&G can help
- Ask for missing info
- Encourage photos
- Offer a free estimate / site visit
- Make the next step easy

## Information To Collect

- Name
- Phone number
- Property address or city
- Type of work needed
- Photos of the area
- Preferred days/times for a site visit
- Timeline or urgency
- Budget range if they mention a large project

## Example Reply

Subject: Re: Free estimate request

Hi [Name],

Thank you for reaching out to D&G Landscape and Masonry. We would be happy to take a look and help you plan the project.

To prepare a free estimate, could you please send:

- The property address or city
- A good phone number
- A few photos of the area
- What you would like done
- The best days or times for a site visit

Once we have that, we can confirm availability and schedule a quick visit.

Thank you,
D&G Landscape and Masonry Inc.
(413) 277-5937

## Safety Rules

- Never promise a final price without a site visit.
- Never claim a date is available unless the calendar confirms it.
- If the customer sounds upset, confused, legal, or high-risk, create a draft and label it for human review.
- If photos are missing, ask for them.
- If a phone number is missing, ask for it.

## Suggested Gmail Labels

- Website Leads
- Needs Human Review
- Draft Reply Ready
- Estimate Scheduled

## Next Build Step

Create an automation that checks Gmail every 10-15 minutes, finds new lead emails, creates a draft reply, and applies labels.
